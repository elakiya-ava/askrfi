3. Please describe your data security protocols to ensure client data is held securely and not shared outside of the immediate project team.
Standard answer.
Information is protected through a comprehensive data security framework that combines technical, administrative, and physical safeguards. Access to information is restricted based on role and business need, using authentication, authorization, and encryption controls to ensure confidentiality and integrity. Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, and systems are monitored continuously for unauthorized access or anomalous activity. Regular security assessments, staff training, and adherence to industry standards (such as ISO 27001, GDPR, and other applicable regulations) ensure that all information is managed and protected to the highest standards of security and compliance.
Long answer.
We take the confidentiality of client data very seriously and we apply diligent and rigorous processes to ensure that we are protecting confidential client information at all times.
Avalere Health is compliant with General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the UK Data Protection Act. All Avalere Health employees are trained on data protection. Each team handling personal data ensures that it is handled and processed in accordance with Avalere Health’s governing policy and data protection legislation including GDPR.
https://avalerehealth.com/privacy-policy/
To protect data at rest, databases, file repositories, and storage devices are protected using strong encryption and complex passwords.
We follow role-based and “least privileged” access standards and ensure passwords are never shared between individuals.
We follow the “minimum necessary” principle for access and data. We maintain audit logs for storing sensitive data, which are routinely monitored.
Sensitive personal data and client-sensitive data or documents are always transferred using a secure File Transfer Protocol (SFTP) or other encrypted channels. Remote access to systems is established over a virtual private network (VPN).
It is prohibited for any user to utilize a mass storage device to house Avalere Health’s or a client’s data.  If the use of a storage device is needed, the device must follow cryptographic control standards and the encryption key / password must be communicated separately and not stored with the device.
Firewalls are contractual, electronic, and physical. All employees sign confidentiality agreements as part of their contracts and are only given access to client confidential information as required for the performance of their duties. Electronic firewalls comprise of complex, dynamic access management on a positive access basis. Access can be restricted at a business unit, company, and project level as required. Physical firewalls protect instances in which teams in the same geographic location provide services to competing clients. Office environments are designed to ensure no inadvertent sharing of materials can take place, including physical separation, secure printing, a clear desk policy, separate meeting rooms, privacy screens, and a code of conduct for staff.
Both our central compliance team and business unit leaders are responsible and accountable for maintaining these critical protections. Whilst we are united as a single organization, we value the need to maintain strict separations in order to safeguard sensitive information at all times.
We recommend clients engage with our information technology security and compliance teams to ensure all requirements are met, particularly those that may be client specific.
We will always partner with our client’s legal and security teams to ensure all solutions always meet the provided client standards.
