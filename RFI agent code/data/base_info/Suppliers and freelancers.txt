1. What type of organization is Avalere Health?
Avalere Health is a private UK-registered corporation. It is not publicly held. 
Source: Recent RFI Response Dec '25. 
Subject owner(s): Michael Hutcheson
2. Describe your organization's legal entity structure.
Avalere Health is an independent, integrated agency. Our group is headed by Avalere
Health Topco Ltd., a privately owned UK-registered entity. All 11 contracting entities
are direct or indirect subsidiaries of topco. These entities facilitate work we conduct
within our core geographies listed above, and preserve existing work contracted
under entities acquired by Avalere Health. 
Source: Recent RFI Responses Dec '25. 
Subject owner(s): Michael Hutcheson
3.  Who owns Avalere Health?
Avalere Health is ultimately owned by Bridgepoint Europe VI Fund, a UK
headquartered private equity investor, listed on the London Stock Exchange. In
addition to Bridgepoint, there are several minority shareholders, including Avalere
Health management. No individual shareholder, other than Bridgepoint, has an
interest greater than 5%.
Source: CRD 1.0. Feb '25.

4.  When was Avalere Health founded?
SHORT: 2001.
LONG: Avalere Health today is the outcome of a 20-year journey. Founded in 2001 as
Fishawack Health, we have grown from a small UK-based Medcomms agency to a
global healthcare commercialization partner, with 1300 industry experts supporting
top-20 pharmaceutical, biopharmaceutical, biotech, and devices manufacturers. Our
teams partner with clients at all stages of the product lifecycle, providing services
from strategy to execution.

6.  Describe any major organizational changes (mergers, acquisitions,
ownership changes) since…
Last 3 years: There have been no major organizational changes since 2022. 
Last 5 years. Beginning in 2001 as a medical communications agency, Avalere Health
has grown organically and via acquisition to become the global healthcare
commercialization partner we are today. In 2021 and 2022, we completed this journey
by expanding our advisory services through the acquisition of PRMA, PAI, and
Avalere to greatly scale our policy, access, value and evidence offering. Alongside, our
acquisition of closerlook augmented our marketing, omnichannel, and media services
in the US domestic market. Avalere Health's ownership has remained unchanged
since March 2020, when UK-based private equity firm Bridgepoint took ultimate
ownership.
Source: CRD 1.0 Feb '25
Subject owner(s): Antony Stewart
