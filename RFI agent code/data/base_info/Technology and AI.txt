2. Describe your AI strategy and organizational approach.
At Avalere Health, intelligence and innovation begin with people. Our curiosity,
creativity, and experience have shaped the way health decisions are understood and
communicated. Our approach to AI is centered around co-intelligence. Co-
intelligence unlocks the full potential of our people by empowering experts with an
integrated and compliant AI toolkit, allowing us to amplify our capabilities, scale our
solutions, enhance quality, and deliver measurable value to our clients, partners, and
patients. Our approach is focused both on developing AI-first products and tools
directly accessible by clients as well as internal solutions and training to elevate and
transform the way our experts deliver their work.
